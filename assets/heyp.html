TT WebApp

This is a simple web app to know the free slots of your friends and yourself. You can create a new time table or join an existing one. You can also view your time table and your friend's time table.

Viewing a time table

  1. Go to View My TT Section {if you do not have a unique TT id, refer the next section}.
  2. Enter your unique TT id.
  3. Press Submit.
And that's it, once you press submit you'll be able to see a of all your friends and yourself.

Creating a new time table

  1. Go to Join/Create Section.
  2. Enter your name and select any emoji of your choice.
  3. Leave the Unique TT ID section blank for now.
  4. Select the slots in your Time Table by double-clicking each slot.
  5. Press Submit.
And that's it, once you press submit you will receive your unique time table key, share this id with your friends to allow them to join your time table.

Joining a time table

  1. Go to Join/Create Section.
  2. Enter your name and select any emoji of your choice.
  3. Fill in the Unique TT ID given by your friend.
  4. Select the slots in your Time Table by double-clicking each slot.
  5. Press Submit.
And that's it, enter the unique ID on the View My TT page and you will be able to see your and your friend's time table.
